About company

Numerical Technologies is the office of the СompatibL company in Kyiv. СompatibL is a leading provider of analytical and computational software, risk modelling and measurement methodology, open and closed source derivatives valuation services for financial industry.

Since 2003, when company was founded by Alexander Sokol, we have been transforming Fintech industry and leveraging performance among our global client base of leading banks, central banks, supranational and asset managers in the US, EMEA and Asia, including 4 out of 5 largest derivatives dealers.

Currently, CompatibL’s offices located in the United States, the United Kingdom, Singapore, Poland and Belarus.
